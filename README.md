# BambooCrash

**Play now**: [bambocrash.onrender.com](https://bambocrash.onrender.com)

**BambooCrash** is an intense, fast-paced 3D multiplayer drone combat game! Fight against other players in real-time, dodge incoming lasers, collect powerups to outmaneuver your opponents, and dominate the scoreboard.

## How to Play

### Controls
* **Movement:** 
  * **PC:** `W`, `A`, `S`, `D` for directional movement. Use `Space` to ascend and `Shift` to descend.
  * **Mobile:** Use the on-screen virtual Joystick.
* **Camera / Look:** 
  * **PC:** Mouse movement.
  * **Mobile:** Swipe on the right side of the screen.
* **Shoot:** 
  * **PC:** `Enter` key. (Features soft aim-assist towards nearby enemies).
  * **Mobile:** On-screen `SHOOT` button.
* **Scoreboard:** Hold `Tab` (or tap the top-left toggle button on mobile).
* **Pause Menu / HUD Editor:** Press `P` (or use the top-right settings button).

### Gameplay Mechanics & Items

* 🟢 **Green Objects (Health):** Restores missing health (+40 HP). Keep yourself alive!
* 🔵 **Blue Objects (Speed):** Grants a temporary extreme speed boost for 3 seconds. Great for escaping or chasing!
* ⚫ **Black Obstacles:** Ramming into these solid pillars will cause crash damage (-10 HP) and momentarily shake your camera.
* 🔴 **Enemy Projectiles:** Watch out for enemy laser fire! Taking a hit deals significant damage (-20 HP).

### Rooms & Matchmaking
When joining the game from the main lobby, you can either play in the default global arena or enter a custom **ROOM CODE**. Entering a room code allows you to play in a private session specifically with your friends!

### Rounds
Every match operates on a 3-minute round system per room. At the end of the countdown, the room's final scores are prominently revealed, and the scoreboard is completely wiped clean before the next round begins!
